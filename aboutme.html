About Me

Where I come from

I was born and raised in British Columbia, Canada. Growing up so close to the mountains, I always look forward to skiing in the winter and hiking in the summer. I also enjoy playing hockey, baseball, and chess.

Where I am now

This Fall I'll be continuing my studies at McGill University, pursuing a degree in software engineering. Throughout my time there, I've loved exploring the city, experiencing the culture, and working on my French!

Something Interesting

Last Winter I had the opportunity to compete in the Quebec Engineering Junior Design Competition with my two friends: Celina and Grady. We stayed up all night designing and building our prototype and ended up placing 4th out of 11 universities. We'll be shooting for gold next year!

Something I'm Proud of

At the end of last summer I competed in the Esprit de Montreal olympic-distance triathlon. This was an event I had been training for all summer and was incredibly proud to complete. I ended up completing the course in 2:49:03. Next stop... Half Ironman!
